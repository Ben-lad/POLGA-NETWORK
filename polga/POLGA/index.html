Guiding Roadmap

We are intentional with our plans and our purposes as an ecosystem in the blockchain space.

1

We aim to bring blockchain and cryptocurrency education to

in local dialects (Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa).

2

Creating the most memorable “Web3 Edu-tainment” hub

3

Providing quality Web3 educational initiatives to various schools,
institutions, entrepreneurs, etc.
